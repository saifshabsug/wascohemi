Refund Policy

Last updated: 2024

Service Nature

Wasco × Hemi Import provides consulting and coordination services for industrial equipment sourcing and import logistics. Our services include research, negotiation, inspection coordination, and logistics facilitation.

Refund Conditions

Refunds may be considered under the following circumstances:

  • Pre-Engagement: Full refund available if requested before supplier engagement begins
  • Service Interruption: Partial refund if we cannot complete agreed services due to our limitations
  • Misrepresentation: Refund considered if our service scope was misrepresented

Non-Refundable Situations:

  • Services already rendered (research, negotiations, inspections completed)
  • Third-party costs already incurred (inspection fees, shipping costs)
  • Supplier-related issues beyond our control
  • Changes in client requirements after engagement begins
  • Market price fluctuations or exchange rate changes

Inspection Disputes

If quality inspection results are disputed:

  • We will coordinate additional inspection if technically feasible
  • Costs for additional inspections are typically shared or borne by the requesting party
  • Final decisions rest with certified inspection companies
  • Refunds for inspection coordination fees are evaluated case-by-case

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Contact us via email or WhatsApp with your request
  2. Provide detailed explanation of the circumstances
  3. Include relevant documentation or correspondence
  4. Allow up to 10 business days for review and response

Processing Timelines

  • Review Process: 5-10 business days
  • Approval Decision: Communicated within 3 business days of review completion
  • Refund Processing: 7-14 business days from approval (depending on payment method)
  • Administrative Fees: May be deducted to cover processing costs

Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with our refund decision, we encourage direct communication to resolve the matter. We are committed to fair and reasonable resolution of all service-related concerns.
